**IRIAM US Illustration Team** (2025-08-19 09:54)
Hi <@845704632881250325>  👋 

Quick update from our team! We've started production on your model and expect to have the first color rough by **9/12**, so please hang on tight 🙏 
Just to go over the changes we can accommodate at each phase:

- **Color Rough:**
Here we can accommodate up to two sets of changes. These can be changes to the clothes themselves, adding smaller additional accessories, or touching up the hair! The biggest changes happen here, so please let us know if there are things you'd like to add once we get the draft to you!

- **Line Art:**
The illustrator will clean up the outlines from the rough draft, allowing for more clarity for smaller details. Here we can adjust the size of accessories and the finer details of clothes! 

- **Final Render:**
The illustrator will color in the outline and render the piece. Here we can adjust the colors, brightness, and saturation, but we cannot make adjustments to the design itself.

We're looking forward to sharing the draft and will be back as soon as possible!
