Through guidance from an experienced mentor and encouragement from fellow participants, you’ll take daily, concrete steps toward growing your presence as a VTuber.

⸻

✅ Participant Requirements
To join the Bootcamp, participants are expected to:
- Stream on IRIAM for at least 30 minutes every day during the program
- Post on X (Twitter) every day
- Actively engage with other VTubers and fans on X through likes, replies, and comments

⸻

📌 How It Works
- Participants will be invited to a dedicated DSP Bootcamp Discord server
- Each participant will receive a private channel for daily missions and progress reports
- You’ll be given starter materials including tips, strategies, and a clear activity schedule
- IRIAM staff and mentors will check in weekly to review progress and provide feedback
- At the end of the month, we’ll look back on your growth together and gather your reflections for future improvement

⸻
